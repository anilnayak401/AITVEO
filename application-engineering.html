Frequently Asked Questions

What technology stacks do you specialize in?
We are tech-agnostic but have deep expertise in modern stacks including Java/Spring, Node.js, Python, React, and Angular, deployed on all major cloud platforms.
How do you handle legacy system modernization?
We use a "strangler pattern" approach, incrementally replacing legacy components with modern services to minimize risk and disruption to your business.
Do you provide ongoing support after deployment?
Yes. We offer tiered managed services and application support to ensure your software remains healthy and performant 24/7.
