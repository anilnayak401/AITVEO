Frequently Asked Questions

How do you ensure the accuracy of Generative AI outputs?
We use advanced RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) techniques and rigorous prompt engineering, combined with human-in-the-loop validation, to minimize hallucinations.
What is Agentic AI and how does it differ from standard AI?
Agentic AI involves autonomous agents that can plan, reason, and use tools to complete multi-step tasks, rather than just providing static responses.
Is our data secure when used with large language models?
Yes. We implement private cloud deployments and strict data masking/anonymization protocols to ensure your proprietary data never leaves your secure environment.
